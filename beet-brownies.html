Ingredients

  • Flour
    1/2 cups gluten free
  • Cocoa Powder
    1/4 cup
  • Sugar
    1/3 cup
  • Baking powder
    1 teaspoon
  • Chocolate chips
    1/3 cup
  • Beet puree
    1/3 cup
  • Eggs
    3, large
  • Coconut oil
    1/4 cup, melted
  • Vanilla
    1 teaspoon

Cook Times

  • Cook time: 27.5 minutes

Tools

  • Baking dish - 9x9
  • Mixing bowl - large
  • Measuring cups / spoons
  • Mixing spoon

Beet Brownies

Directions

Preheat oven to 350˚F. Grease a 9x9 baking dish.

 

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the: 1 1/4 cups gluten free flour, 1/4 cup cocoa powder, 1/3 cup sugar, 1 teaspoon baking powder, 1/3 cup chocolate chips

 

Add the: 1/3 cup beet puree, 3 large eggs, 1/4 cup coconut oil (melted), and 1 teaspoon vanilla.

 

Transfer batter into greased baking dish and cook at 350˚F for 27.5 minutes, or until it passes the clean toothpick test.
