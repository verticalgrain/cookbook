Ingredients

  • Flour
    1¼ cups all-purpose
  • Brown Sugar
    2 tbsp.
  • Baking Powder
    2 tsp.
  • Salt
    ¼ tsp.
  • Cinnamon
    ¾ tsp. ground
  • Nutmeg
    ¼ tsp. ground
  • Ginger
    Dash, ground
  • Ground cloves
    Dash
  • Milk
    1 cup
  • Pumpkin puree
    ½ cup
  • Egg
    1 large
  • Coconut oil
    2 tbsp.
  • Blueberries
    ¼ cup, frozen or fresh

Tools

  • Mixing bowl - medium
  • Measuring cups / spoons
  • Mixing spoon
  • Mixing bowl - small
  • Griddle, skillet or pan
  • Spatula
  • Baking sheet

Pumpkin blueberry pancakes

Directions

Preheat oven to 200˚F.

In a medium mixing bowl, whisk together the 1¼ cups flour, 1 tbsp. sugar, 2 tsp. baking powder, ¼ tsp. salt, ¾ tsp. cinnamon, ¼ tsp. nutmeg, dash ginger.

In a small mixing bowl, combine the 1 cup milk, ½ cup pumpkin, 1 egg and 2 tbsp. oil or butter.

Stir into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.

Pour batter on a greased griddle or skillet heated to medium heat (325˚F). The pancakes won't spread much, so use the back of a spoon to shape into a circle if desired. Sprinkle blueberries on top. Flip when bubbles begin forming on top. Cook until golden brown.

Place pancakes on the baking sheet and pop in the oven to warm until served.

Serve with maple syrup, fruit, or whatever you like.
